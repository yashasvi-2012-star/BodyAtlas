About BodyAtlas

BodyAtlas is an interactive medical visualization platform designed to help students, educators, healthcare professionals and curious minds explore the human body in ways traditional textbooks cannot. From anatomy exploration to disease simulations and medicine pathways, BodyAtlas transforms complex biological systems into immersive and engaging experiences.
