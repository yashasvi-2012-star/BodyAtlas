## what is body atlas ?
 BodyAtlas is a modern interactive platform built to make learning human anatomy and health actually intreresting . explore the human body like never before throught clickable organs and body systems discover how they work and learn about diseases medicines, surguries , medical tests , biological processes in a simple and visual ways . wheather your a student , a curious learner or someone intreasted in healthcare (or probably here to just review my project !!) , bodyAtlas helps you understand the human body without getting lost in complicated medical terms . with detailed organ informations , quiz, compition mode , animated videos , educational resourses and a intutive dashboard-style interface , BodyAtlas is your go to destination for all things related to human anatomy and health . it turn learning into engaging and immensive experience that will leave you with a deeper understanding of the human body and how it works AND IT ALSO HAVE ANIMTED VIDEOS FOR BETTER UNDERSTANDING !!
