# BodyAtlas
an interactive learning app for human body where you can see the human body on your screen and interact with it Most people know:
🩸"The heart pumps blood."

🫁"The lungs help you breathe."
"Insulin helps diabetes."

But very few people understand:

⚡How blood actually moves through the body.
⚡Why a heart attack happens.
⚡What asthma does inside the lungs.
⚡How medicines travel and work.

An interactive visual explanation can make those concepts click instantly.
you can explore
⚡the Nervous system
⚡Digestive system
⚡Respiratory system
⚡Circulatory system
⚡Skeletal system
AND YOU CAN EVEN !!!!
Click a disease and see:

⚡What changes inside the body
⚡Symptoms
⚡Risk factors
⚡Prevention

Examples:

⚡Diabetes
⚡Asthma
⚡Pneumonia
⚡Heart attack
⚡Kidney stones AND  ALSO !!!!!
Select a medicine and watch:

⚡Enter the body
⚡Get absorbed
⚡Travel in blood
⚡Reach target organ
⚡Produce effects

Examples:

⚡Paracetamol
⚡Antibiotics
⚡Insulin
⚡Inhalers AND THERE ARE ALSO!!!
⚡Story-based animations:

🫁 Journey of Oxygen

🍕 Journey of Food

💉 Journey of a Vaccine

🩸 Journey of Blood

⚡ Journey of a Nerve Signal 
AND IT ALSO HAVE A SPECIAL FEATURE !!! ⚡TO TOGGLE THE ORGAN IN DIFFRENT MODE (HEALTHY OR DISEASE (SELECTED))!!!!!!⚡
